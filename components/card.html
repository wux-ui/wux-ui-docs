WuX-UI Card

The Card component is used as a basic container and is available in both flat and floating styles.

Example

Hello! 
<div class="wux-card">
  <div class="wux-card-header">
    <img src="https://image.moeblog.gq/i/2022/07/16/nsdzkk.svg">
  </div>
  <div class="wux-card-body">
    Hello!
  </div>
  <div class="wux-card-footer">
    <button class="wux-btn wux-btn-primary wux-btn-text">Test</button>
  </div>
</div>

Flat

To apply the Card's Flat style, simply apply the wux-card-flat style next to the wux-card.
