WuX-UI Tables Beta

The Tables component is used to display multiple profiles in a neat and easy-to-read manner.

Example

# A B C
1 AwA OwO QwQ
2 QwQ AwA OwO
3 OwO QwQ AwA 
<table class="wux-table wux-table-fluid">
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>#</th>
      <th>A</th>
      <th>B</th>
      <th>C</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <th>1</th>
      <td>AwA</td>
      <td>OwO</td>
      <td>QwQ</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th>2</th>
      <td>QwQ</td>
      <td>AwA</td>
      <td>OwO</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th>3</th>
      <td>OwO</td>
      <td>QwQ</td>
      <td>AwA</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>
