WuX-UI Dropdown

The Dropdown component is used to hold less important content in a limited space.

Example

  • User
  • My Homepage
  • My Project
  • My Stars
  • Close
 
<span class="wux-dropdown">
  <button class="wux-btn wux-dropdown-trigger">
    Dropdown!
  </button>
  <ul class="wux-dropdown-menu">
    <li class="wux-dropdown-menu-header">User</li>
    <li class="wux-dropdown-item">My Homepage</li>
    <li class="wux-dropdown-item">My Project</li>
    <li class="wux-dropdown-item">My Stars</li>
    <li class="wux-dropdown-menu-divider"></li>
    <li class="wux-dropdown-item">Close</li>
  </ul>
</span>
